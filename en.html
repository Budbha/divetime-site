About

DiveTime — a place where depth feels closer

We help people take their first step into diving without fear or pressure. During the lessons, the instructor explains everything in simple words: how the equipment works, how to breathe, how to behave in the water and what to do if you feel unsure.

DiveTime is suitable for those who have never tried diving before, want to feel more confident in the water or plan to prepare for open water dives.

FAQ

Common beginner questions

The most important thing is that you can start calmly. The instructor will be nearby and explain everything.

Can I join without experience?

Yes. A trial lesson is designed exactly for people who have never tried diving before.

Is it safe?

Yes, if training takes place with an instructor and the rules are followed. Everything is done step by step during the lesson.

Do I need to swim well?

You do not need to be an athlete. Basic comfort in the water and listening to the instructor are enough.

What should I bring?

Usually you need swimwear, a towel, slippers and a good mood. We will clarify the rest before the lesson.

From what age can you start?

The age depends on the lesson format and club rules. It is best to ask when booking.

How long does a lesson last?

A trial lesson usually takes around 60–90 minutes, including explanation and preparation.

Do I need my own equipment?

No. For a trial lesson and training, the club usually provides the equipment.
